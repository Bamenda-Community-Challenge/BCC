About B.C.C

The Bamenda community challenge commonly known as the BCC is an annual event that brings out great young minds in Bamenda who are working on products for the development of the community,.This event brings out great minds from various engineering fields in Bamenda. Most of these young innovators are students who are based in Bamenda and who have an interest in showing the world what Bamenda is made of and building the Bamenda tech community. The first edition of BCC was launched last year and the demo day was held on the 4th of June.

Build a solution to a local problem in our community using tech skills , in accordance with one or more of the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals.

Step 1:

Create a team

Create a team of a maximum of 5 members with atleast 2 girls

Step 2:

Find a problem in your society you can solve using IT skills

Locate a problem in your community which falls under the United Nations Sustainable development goals and solve this problem using IT skills.

Step 3:

Register and submit your project

Register your team and submit your projects via the BCC official website

Timeline

1

Registration Begins

10th March - 25th March

Teams will register their projects via the official BCC website, where they will submit details about their team and their project details including a 3-minute video showing a demo of their project.

2

Announcement of top 16 teams

6th of April

Judges will go through the submission files and 16 teams will be selected. This teams will have 2 - 3 weeks to work on thier projects and improve the functionality of thier projects and later on do a resubmision of the updates of thier project.

3

Preselection of top 8 teams

15th of April

The top 16 teams will have a preselection phase where they will pitch and demo thier idea to judges and later on 8 teams will be selected

4

Top 8 teams results released

22nd of April

Results of the top 8 teams will be annouced and this teams will prepare for the denmo day in june

5

Demo Day

3th of June

Top 8 teams will present thier projects to the entire Bamenda Community where the will pitch and compete to become the champions of Bamenda.
